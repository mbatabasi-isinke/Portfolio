🌱 EcoGrid Pavement

Project Overview

EcoGrid Pavement is a STEM innovation that converts footsteps into electricity using a modular bamboo kinetic energy floor. The generated energy can power school security lights and other low-power devices.

Problem It Solves

  • Limited electricity in schools
  • High energy costs
  • Wasted walking energy
  • Need for clean and renewable energy

Key Features

  • 🌿 Eco-friendly bamboo design
  • ⚡ Converts footsteps into electricity
  • 💡 Powers security lights
  • ♻️ Renewable energy source
  • 🏫 Designed for schools and public walkways
